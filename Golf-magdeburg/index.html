Greenoic golf at Flora Park
Garden

Our 18-hole course is spaciously located in a beautifully
landscaped green area at the Flora Park garden and is
approx. 200 m away from the Flora Park Magdeburg. Mini
golf is the ideal leisure sport for the whole family. Children
are especially welcome. Since exercise and fresh air whet
your appetite, we offer you snacks, ice cream and drinks.
You can borrow the equipment for playing from us. Of
course, we give you tips on how to play the game.

Try mini golf and you'll see that adults can have fun too.
Afterwards you can evaluate the results comfortably in the
beer garden and toast to victory or defeat together.

Mini golf gameplay

Mini golf is a sport that can be completed in a team, family
or alone. The aim of the game is to get the ball into the
hole in as few strokes as possible using the bat. This is
theoretically possible on the tracks of all standardized
systems with a single shot, an ace. The tracks differ in their
degree of difficulty due to different obstacles.

Tips from the pro

Everyone can play minigolf after a short instruction.
However, if you want to acquire more comprehensive
knowledge and techniques, you can take advantage of our
beginner training. Experienced trainers show tricks
and tips on how you can compete professionally in every
tournament in the future.
Please make an appointment for the training session in
advance.
