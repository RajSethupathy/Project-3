Pause in a typical beer garden
atmosphere

Enjoy our numerous delicacies with a tasty beer from the barrel
under the shady chestnut trees. In addition to a typical snack,
Viennese and Bockwurst with bread, fries, currywurst, we also offer
delicious Soljanka. Even from a distance, the sound of the beer
mugs suggests the Bavarian “live and let live”.

In our beer garden you not only eat and drink well, you also sit very
comfortably. Numerous tables and beer benches with comfortable
backrests invite you to linger. In addition to our range of beers, we
also offer various soft drinks and selected, well-tempered wines
from Germany. Or simply stop by for a coffee or an ice cream.

Surrounded by fresh green, our beer garden is ideal for company
and family celebrations. Upon request, we will be happy to prepare
fresh Bavarian delicacies and/or a delicious barbecue buffet for you.
In addition to refreshments, we offer popular small snacks every
day.

Fresh beer enjoyment

Look forward to our beer specialties from the Veldenstein private
brewery again this year .

Neuhaus an der Pegnitz is located between Nuremberg
and Bayreuth, in the heart of the Franconian Switzerland-Veldenstein
Forest Nature Park.

These beer specialties have been brewed there since 1929 in the
spirit of the medieval Veldenstein Castle, following the good old
brewing tradition. When selecting the ingredients, only the best and
strictly controlled ingredients from the region are used. Every step
in the brewing process is accompanied by the master brewers and
brewers with the utmost care.

The Veldenstein beer specialties have achieved top results in the
DLG beer test!
